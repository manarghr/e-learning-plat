Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us or check our FAQ section below.


How do I enroll in a course?

Enrolling in a course is simple! Browse our course catalog, select the course you're interested in, and click the "Enroll" button. If it's a free course, you'll get immediate access. For premium courses, you'll be directed to a payment page to complete your enrollment.

Are the certificates recognized by employers?

Yes! Our certificates are recognized by many employers worldwide. We've partnered with industry leaders to ensure our curriculum meets professional standards, making our certificates valuable additions to your resume.

Can I access the courses on mobile devices?

Our platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones and tablets. You can download our mobile app for an even better experience with offline access to course materials.

How do I become a mentor on E-Taalim?

To become a mentor, click on the "Join as Mentor" button and fill out the application form. Our team will review your credentials and experience. If approved, you'll receive guidance on creating and publishing your first course.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied with a course?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for most courses. If you're not satisfied with the content, you can request a refund within 30 days of purchase, provided you haven't completed more than 30% of the course.
